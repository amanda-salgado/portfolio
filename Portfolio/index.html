About Me

I'm so grateful that I found the Tech-Moms program! I have learned so much and have gained the confidence to be able to continue learning and exploring technology. I'm still figuring out which direcion I want to go, but I'm excited about all of the opportunities that are available through Tech-Moms.

I am a mom to three awesome, crazy, kids (a teenager, a preteen, and a preschooler), and I have been married to my best friend for 16 years. I am also a Multilingual Literacy teacher at Ogden High School, and I get to know and teach students from all over the world. I love to travel, learn languages, read, eat good food, explore technology, and be outside. I hope to someday start a career in tech that will let me combine all of those interests and have more time to do the things I like to do while still connecting with people and making a difference.
